About

An operating system for thinking about AI

What this is

Most AI courses teach you to build AI by hand — they make AI engineers. This one teaches you to think about AI as a system: how to understand it, classify the tools, make the right architecture and governance decisions, and then direct AI coding tools (Claude, Cursor, Codex) to do the hands-on building for you.

It makes AI architects, operators, and decision-makers — and it's free.

Who it's for

Founders, executives, consultants, product and operations managers, lawyers, accountants and other domain experts, IT managers, analysts — and engineers who want the systems, operations, and governance lens. No coding required.

The five-verb loop

Every lesson follows the same spine: Understand → Classify → Decide → Apply → Govern. That's what turns topics into judgment.

An honest scope

We will help you spec, prototype, evaluate, govern, and direct AI-built systems — and recognise when a system needs a real engineer before production. We do not pretend a non-coder can ship secure production software alone; AI-generated code frequently carries security and maintainability issues, and we teach you where those limits are.

How we keep it trustworthy

AI changes monthly, and some of this material (governance, compliance, security) carries real stakes. So:

  • Primary sources. Governance, compliance, and tooling claims link to primary sources, listed at the foot of each lesson.
  • "Last reviewed" dates. Every lesson shows when it was last checked, so you can judge freshness.
  • Volatile specifics are isolated in dated cards, separate from the durable concepts — so they can be refreshed without rewriting lessons.
  • Open corrections. Anyone — coder or not — can suggest a fix. Corrections are tracked publicly.
This content is for education only. It is not legal, compliance, financial, or other professional advice. Governance and regulatory guidance is general and sector-aware, not jurisdiction-definitive — always verify against primary sources and consult a qualified professional for your situation.

Sustainability

Free forever — no paywalls, no accounts, no ads. Keeping it accurate takes ongoing work; if it helps you, sponsoring the project funds that maintenance. We also keep a public roadmap and changelog.
