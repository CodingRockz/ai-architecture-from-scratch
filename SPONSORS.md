# Sponsors

**AI Architecture From Scratch is free, and always will be.**

There are no paywalls, no accounts required, and no ads. But keeping the content accurate
in a field that changes every month — re-checking tools, prices, model capabilities, and
regulations — takes ongoing work.

If this project helps you or your team, sponsoring it directly funds that maintenance.

## Ways to support

- **Sponsor the project** — _(funding links to be added: e.g. GitHub Sponsors, Open Collective)_
- **Spread the word** — share a lesson with someone who's "AI-curious but not technical."
- **Improve the content** — even non-coders can [suggest fixes](contribute.html); fresh
  tool and regulation info is genuinely valuable.

## What sponsorship funds

- Quarterly review and refresh of tool cards, model capabilities, pricing, and regulations.
- New lessons and tracks.
- Accessibility, translations (i18n), and site improvements.

## Transparency

We keep a public [roadmap](ROADMAP.md) and [changelog](CHANGELOG.md) so you can see exactly
what's being worked on and what your support enables.

> Sponsorship supports maintenance and does **not** buy influence over technical or
> governance guidance — that stays independent and primary-source-backed.
