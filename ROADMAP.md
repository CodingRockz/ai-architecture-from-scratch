## What's coming

- **Keeping it fresh** — tools, model capabilities, prices, and regulations change monthly;
  these specifics live in dated cards and are refreshed regularly.
- **More visuals** — additional diagrams and worked examples in the denser lessons.
- **Translations (i18n)** — the lesson structure already supports other languages.
- **Deeper learner support** — richer examples and recaps to keep the path easy to follow.

## Want to shape it?

You don't need to know Git. Non-coders can use the
[suggestion / correction form](https://codingrockz.github.io/ai-architecture-from-scratch/contribute.html);
developers can open an issue or pull request. Spotting an out-of-date tool or a confusing
explanation is genuinely valuable.
